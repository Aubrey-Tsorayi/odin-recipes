Sadza

Description

This a tradional meal made in the country of Zimbabwe. One of main meals of a family

It is easy to cook, even kids can follow along.

Ingredinets

  • Mealie Meal
  • Water

Steps

  1. Put half a pot of water
  2. boil water for 5 minutes
  3. Add a cup of Mealie meal
  4. Mix until you have a thick texture (Add half cup of mealie meal is texture not thick)
  5. Let it boil for 5 minutes
  6. Add another cup of mealie meal and mix
  7. Mix till it thickness
  8. When it thickenes leave on the stove for 2 mintues
  9. Ready to serve
