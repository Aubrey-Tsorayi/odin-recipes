Beef Stew

Description

Beef stew is a hearty dish of tender beef chunks simmered in a flavorful broth with vegetables like potatoes, carrots, and sometimes onions. It's a classic comfort food, perfect for a cozy meal.

Ingredinets

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat (cut into 1-inch cubes)
  • 1 ½ teaspoons kosher salt
  • ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 large yellow onion (cut into chunks)

Steps

  1. Season beef stew meat with 1 ½ teaspoons kosher salt and ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
  3. Add seasoned beef and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Remove beef from the pot and set aside.
  5. Add chopped onion to the pot and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
