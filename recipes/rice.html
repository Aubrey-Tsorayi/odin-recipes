Rice

Description

Rice is a cereal grain, and in its domesticated form is the staple food for over half of the world's human population, particularly in Asia and Africa, due to the vast amount of soil that is able to grow rice.

Ingredinets

  • Rice
  • Water

Steps

  1. Put 2 cups of rice in a pot
  2. Put 4 cups of water in a pot
  3. boil water rice
  4. Taste rice for soft texture
  5. If not yet soft and water is finished add another half cup of water
  6. If texture is soft. its ready to serve
